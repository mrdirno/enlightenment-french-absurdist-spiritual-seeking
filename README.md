## Entry Points for Contributors

**Start anywhere. Fork freely. No permission needed.**

### Story Entry Points

- Write a scene where a minor municipal bureaucrat experiences a genuine, fleeting moment of grace while processing Form S-74.
- Create the 'Ferret's Secret'—the story behind the photograph in the concierge's booth, revealing an absurd, tiny spiritual lineage.
- Draft a chapter where Gaspard attempts to get a 'signed testimonial to profound inner change' from the repulsive salesman on the third floor.
- Imagine Bernard the waiter's internal monologue as he serves coffee to seekers, observing their failed epiphanies with celestial detachment.

### Technical Entry Points

- Build a 'Soul Weight Class' calculator that asks absurd, philosophical questions and outputs a bureaucratic classification.
- Create an interactive map of the 'Rue de l'Espérance' where users can click on buildings (café, police station, ministry) to reveal vignettes of existential delay.
